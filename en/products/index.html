Rainbow Trout

Finland's most loved food fish.

We grow rainbow trout under carefully monitored conditions to a sales size of approximately 2.5 kilograms over two growing seasons. Rainbow trout is valued for its versatility, omega-3 fatty acids, vitamin D and protein, making it suitable for food industry use, retail and professional kitchens.

02 / 03

Whitefish

An elegant Nordic classic.

Our whitefish is characterized by low-fat, high-protein white flesh. We raise whitefish to a sales size of 0.4-1 kilograms under controlled conditions and deliver it fresh for customers who need consistent Nordic fish quality.

03 / 03

Rainbow Trout Roe

A seasonal delicacy from autumn to winter.

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids and vitamin B12, rainbow trout roe is both flavorful and nutritious. We deliver roe sacks to clients for further processing, supporting freshness and quality for the commercial sector.
